House Amenities

House Amenities

Everything you need for a comfortable stay.

Laundry

Iron & Board are in the master bedroom wardrobe. The washer/dryer is in the kitchen yard area (detergent pods provided).

Linens & Towels

Extra towels and bed linens are stored in the TV console drawers. Please place used linens in the hamper.

Waste Disposal

Chute is located in the common corridor. Please bag all trash securely before disposal. Recyclables go in the blue bin.

Climate Control

AC remotes are mounted on the wall in each room. For hot water, switch on the heater 15 mins prior (switch next to bathroom light).
